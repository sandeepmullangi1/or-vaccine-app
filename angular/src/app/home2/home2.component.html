COVID-19 Vaccine Eligibility

Before we begin, here is some important information:

What is the information will we ask for?

We will ask questions about yourself, like your age, gender, race, and ethnicity. We will also ask questions about your health, like your history of COVID-19 infections, testing, or vaccinations.

Please know we will never ask you for your bank account number, password, or other financial information.

Can this tool help you sign up for vaccination notifications for someone else?

Yes. You will have the option to create a profile and register for vaccination updates on behalf of someone else, such as a child, parent, or client who is under your care.

Do you need help using this tool?

Please call 2-1-1 [contact information here] and an operator will help you through this process.
