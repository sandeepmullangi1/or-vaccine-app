Oregon COVID-19 Vaccine Screening and Location Finder

Welcome to the Oregon COVID-19 Vaccine Locator

A COVID-19 vaccine is the best way to keep yourself, your family, and your community healthy

Please complete a brief questionnaire to find out when you are eligible for a COVID-19 vaccine.We will not share your information with anyone.

Everyone who wants to be vaccinated will receive a COVID-19 vaccine over the coming months. Due to limited supply, we need to provide the vaccine in phases. We are starting with people who are most at risk.

If you are eligible for the vaccine, this tool will help you find a vaccination location.

If you are not eligible yet, please provide your contact information. We will send important updates on COVID-19 vaccinations and notify you when you become eligible for your vaccine.

Please be advised that the information you provide will be confidential and secure following federal privacy standards.
